Steps to Install Wireless Router

Step 1

Connect your Modem to the telphone wall jack

Step 2

Connect your local router to the local modem

Step 3

Connect your computing device via an ethernet cable to the local router

Step 4

Wait until your modem cycles and then power on the modem

Step 5

Wait until the power LED light turns green and then power on router

Step 5

Insert your installation CD and click router setup, then follow instructions
